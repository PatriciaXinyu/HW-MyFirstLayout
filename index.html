About Me
Hello! My name is Xinyu Huang. You can call me Patricia.
I am a senior Art Practice major from UC Berkeley. I am interested in UI/UX, especially the user research part. I have partnered with Lawrence Hall of Science as an innovative designer to improve one of their Design Quest exhibits. I am also a freelance artist. I am interested in creating large drawings using graphite and pastel, paper sculptures, and photography. My art explores mental health issues through various representations of human body.
